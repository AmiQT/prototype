Project Background

Student Talent Profiling App (UTHM)

  • A comprehensive Talent Management System specifically designed for Universiti Tun Hussein Onn Malaysia.
  • Integrates a Flutter Mobile App for students and an Astro Web Dashboard for administrators.
  • Aims to transition from traditional manual record-keeping to a centralized digital ecosystem.
  • Leverages Google Gemini AI to provide automated insights and talent forecasting.

Problem Statement

Current Challenges

  • Fragmented Data: Student achievements and extracurricular records are scattered across various formats.
  • Manual Tracking: High administrative burden in verifying certificates and compiling reports.
  • Invisibility of Talent: Exceptional student skills often go unnoticed due to lack of real-time visualization.
  • Reactive Intervention: Difficulty in identifying "at-risk" students early for effective support.
Presentation 2026 | Student Talent Profiling

Proposed Solution

AI-Driven Talent Ecosystem

  • Centralized Digital Portfolio: A single platform for students to store and showcase verified achievements.
  • Agentic AI Integration: Using Gemini AI to automatically parse certificates and suggest skill tags.
  • Real-time Analytics: Instant dashboard visualization of talent distribution and CGPA trends.
  • Automated Reporting: One-click PDF generation for administrative reviews and risk analysis.
